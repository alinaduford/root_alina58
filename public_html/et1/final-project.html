FINAL PROJECT

My plan is to make a female character that will be idle/slow moving, similar to the lofi girl. There will be a scene, either in her room or in a park where she is doing something quiet/peaceful. The background sound will be calm music.

Lofi vibe, purple/pink lighting. Girl model tutorial, room tutorial, added plants tutorials. Lofi music, possible animation. Chibi/spherical style; simplistic.

Some challenges I might face during this project is with the workload amount. I might get overwhelmed at some points because I will not have a teamate to help me. During this project I will have to meet deadlines and ask for help from teachers or classmates when it is needed.

My capi-goal in this project will be "presenting visual art". This task pushes me to analyze, interpret, and select artistic work for presentation, to use artistic techniques to present artwork effectively, and to convey meaning through the presentation of artistic work.
